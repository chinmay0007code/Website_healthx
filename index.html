How to Use HealthX

Welcome to HealthX, your AI-powered health assistant. Follow these steps to get the most out of our platform:

1. Chat with AI

  • Click on the "Chat with AI" button.
  • Enter your symptoms in the chat.
  • The AI will analyze your input and predict the top 3 possible diseases with a probability score.
  • Always consult a medical professional for confirmation.

2. Generate a Medical Report

  • Click on the "Medical Report" button.
  • Provide details about your symptoms and health history.
  • The AI will generate a report including:
    • Potential disease explanations
    • Recommended workouts & exercises
    • Dietary suggestions
    • Lifestyle changes
    • Preventive measures
  • Use this as a guide but always seek professional medical advice.

3. Stay Updated

HealthX is in beta, and we are continuously improving. Keep checking for updates!
